    Top Tier Programs

    BVCA Program

    BVCA Anyone may do any of these plans but for the top group you must be approved. Fast-paced training designed for players seeking to compete at the next level both as a junior player and collegiately. Small training group sizes with Juniors Sand head coaches. Players are expected to already have mastered the fundamentals.

    $1300 Summer Session Jersey Package not Included

    Competitive Program

    Competitive level teams are created to have high-energy and engaging training designed for the following players: players starting their path to competitive training and national competition, players learning the Juniors Sand training methodology, players who love to train in the sand but have limited time or desire to compete, or dual-sport athletes (i.e indoor volleyball). Limited training group sizes with Juniors Sand coaching staff. Will be playing for a bid to go to nationals. Does not have to accept bid and go.

    $1050 Summer Session Jersey Package not Included or Nationals
